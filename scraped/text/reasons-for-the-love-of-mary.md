## TEN REASONS FOR THE LOVE OF MARY

**1\. INCARNATION :** She gave birth to our Lord & Saviour Jesus Christ (Luke 1:35, Matt 1:18).

**2\. EPIPHANY:** She presented the child Jesus to the world through the Magi (Matt 2:11).

**3\. DOCTOR OF GRACE:** She is full of grace & its most perfect exemplar (Lk 1:28).

**4\. EFFECTIVE INTERCESSOR** : She intervenes at the ordinary and also at the most critical moments (cf Jn 2:3 – Son, they have no wine).

**5\. SHE SEARCHES FOR US WHEN WE ARE LOST IN SIN**

**6\. HER UNIQUE RELATIONSHIP WITH THE BLESSED TRINITY:** (Daughter of the Father, Mother of the Son & the Spouse of the Holy Spirit).

**7\. THE LAST GIFT OF JESUS CHRIST TO HUMANITY**: (the Daughter of Zion and the new Eve, the Woman who was made the mother of all the living by the power of the Holy Spirit, Jn 19:27).

**8\. QUEEN & WOMAN OF PEACE & MOTHER OF MERCY:** (cf. Her apparitions Fatima, Lourdes etc).

**9\. A WOMAN OF THE WORD:** (She is both the Mother of the Word and a woman of the Scriptures. She lives by the Word of God daily).

**10\. A WOMAN OF PROFOUND GRATITUDE:** Grace is her attitude. She lives entirely and gratefully for God. She lives the magnificat and she is the perfect icon of grace-attitude, cf. Lk 1:46 -55.

**\* Fundamentally,** I love Mary because Jesus Christ was the first to do so, and in order to belong to the generation that calls her blessed Lk 1:48

Given by His Grace, The Most Reverend Valerian Okeke, the Archbishop of Onitsha, at Onitsha 9th February, 2017 at the inauguration of  Onitsha Archdiocesan  Marian Year.
