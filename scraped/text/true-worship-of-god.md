## TRUE WORSHIP OF GOD

True worship of God does not only consist of external rituals but stems from inner conversion of hearts to Christ.

Hence, empty religiosity that exalts human traditions only replaces the sincere practice of religion to the detriment of good social order.

Therefore, my dear once, always strive go beyond mere externals to the basic Christian ethos. I invoke upon you God’s abundant blessings.
