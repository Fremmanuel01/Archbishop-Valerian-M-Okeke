## PRAYER IS THE KEY.

Prayer is the key that unlocks all possibilities. Since many are the Amalekites (enemies of our true good) on our journey to the promise land, prayer should be on-going. For a Christian, prayer should be definitive of his/her life. It is lived out in one’s legitimate duties. Pray ceaselessly and work tirelessly.

Pray as though everything depends on God and work as though everything depends on you. God always answers the prayer of faith made persistently to Him. While prayer oils the wheel of Christian living, faith is the oil with which the lamp of prayer burns. Thus steadfastness in prayer made with faith and requisite effort marks the secret of success and victory.

Surely, faith has to be formed and sustained in the word of God. Therefore, focus your attention in building up of strong faith through a consistent life of prayer and scripture with the knowledge that Quitters never win, Winners never quit. With grateful heart I pray for you and all your loved ones.
