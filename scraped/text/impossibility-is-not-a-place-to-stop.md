## IMPOSSIBILITY IS NOT A PLACE TO STOP

IMPOSSIBILITY is not a place to stop. It is a starting point to overcome. Since God of possibilities is at your right hand nothing shall you fear.

His favours rest on you by His special grace. Any wonder His mercy is boundless and His love, deathless.

My regards to you and your loved ones. May God prosper the work of your hand today and always. I wish you God’s favours.
