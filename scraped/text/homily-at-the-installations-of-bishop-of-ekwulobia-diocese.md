# Homily at the installations of Bishop of Ekwulobia Diocese

[Facebook](https://web.facebook.com/Archbishopval) [Twitter](https://twitter.com/AbvalOkeke) [Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC4TqRLRrsoDYMXNn3vLEERQ)

-   [June 12, 2020](https://archbishopvalokeke.org/2020/06/12/)
-   [No Comments](https://archbishopvalokeke.org/homily-at-the-installations-of-bishop-of-ekwulobia-diocese/#respond)

The biblical figure of Moses can serve as an inspiration to the bishop. He was a committed pastor, a courageous leader and a prayer person, a true pontifex, a bridge and mediator between God and his people always returning to the people with a radiant face after his dialogue with the Lord ….the Bishop is called to enter the mysterious yet luminous cloud of the mystery of the Blessed Trinity, the Bishop will be the sign of the mercy of the Father, the living image of the Son in pastoral charity, a man who radiates the light of the Holy Spirit, a homo spiritualis.
