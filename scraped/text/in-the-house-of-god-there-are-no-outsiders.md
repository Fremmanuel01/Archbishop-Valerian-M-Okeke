## IN THE HOUSE OF GOD THERE ARE NO OUTSIDERS

In the house of God there are no outsiders, says the Archbishop, Most Rev. Valerian Okeke. He assures us that God is calling all of us as missionaries, as His real children.

Nobody is left out. And once you are really a missionary by living a new life and living a disciplined life, God will make you his instrument, God will make you a sign of His blessing.

Wherever you are, peace, happiness and fulfilment will follow you. God will use you to bless his people, He will use you to make their faith in Him stronger in him and you who is His instrument of blessing will have more blessing come upon you.
