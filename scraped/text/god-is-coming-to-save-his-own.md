## GOD IS COMING TO SAVE HIS PEOPLE

God is coming to save His people and establish them in new life of joy and blessings. This transformation sustains our hope.

Therefore, have confidence in the Lord as we wait patiently for the day of His coming. Our patience should not be empty and sterile rather it should be filled with hope and humility.

I urge you to emulate John’s spirit of letting go, courage, and thus be the Voice that gave the Word.
