# Solemnity of the Most Holy Body and Blood of Christ 2020

[Facebook](https://web.facebook.com/Archbishopval) [Twitter](https://twitter.com/AbvalOkeke) [Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC4TqRLRrsoDYMXNn3vLEERQ)

-   [June 17, 2020](https://archbishopvalokeke.org/2020/06/17/)
-   [No Comments](https://archbishopvalokeke.org/solemnity-of-the-most-holy-body-and-blood-of-christ-2020/#respond)

Christ shared himself as bread to many according to needs. To the sinner like Mary Magdalene – Bread of forgiveness; To the widow of Nain, Mary, and Martha – Bread of comfort, sympathy and miracle of faith; To Zachaeus – Hunger for God and faith; To the repentant thief – Eternal life in Paradise; To the blind – Sight; To all of us; rich and poor, healthy or sick, white or black – The gift of himself in the Eucharist.
